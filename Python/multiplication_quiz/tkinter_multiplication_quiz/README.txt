=Multiplication Quiz=

A program where it tests the user on one digit multiplication. 10 questions will be asked.

<How to play>
1. Click the "Start" button or press the "Enter" key to start the quiz.

2. The question will appear on the window. Enter your answer in the textbox.

3. To submit your answer, click the "Answer" button or press the enter key.

4. Once all questions are answered, your score will be displayed.
